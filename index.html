Dig: Deep Descent

Story

You finally scraped together enough funds to purchase a modest mining vessel. After months of dull flight, you've arrived at the Outer Belt. You hope to be able to sell enough minerals to acquire 1 billion Credits. That would be enough to secure a coveted berth on an Interstellar Fleet ship. You hope to finally escape this wretched, war-torn system for the promise of Proxima Centauri b and beyond. Besides your mining vessel, you have made contact with the OBTL (Outer Belt Trading League), who will buy minerals you recover. They will also provide upgrades, repairs, and fast-charging to your ship's batteries -- just be sure to make it back to the asteroid's surface in time!

Controls

  • Lateral movement: WASD or arrows
  • Dig down: C
  • Dig up: <space>
  • Quick-Scan: Q

Requirements

Best played on a desktop or laptop with at least 1920x1080 resolution
