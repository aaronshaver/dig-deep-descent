Dig: Deep Descent

pre-alpha build 0.1

Story

  • You finally scraped together enough funds to purchase a modest mining vessel. After months of uneventful flight, you've arrived at the Outer Belt.
  • Your goal is to sell enough minerals to acquire 1 billion Credits. That would be enough to secure a coveted berth on an Interstellar Fleet ship. You hope to finally escape this wretched, war-torn system for the promise of Proxima Centauri b and beyond.
  • You made contact with the OBTL (Outer Belt Trading League), who will buy minerals you recover. They will also provide upgrades, repairs, and fast-charging to your ship's batteries -- just be sure to make it back to the surface in time.
  • As you dig deeper, more valuable minerals will appear, but so will more danger!

Controls

  • Lateral movement / dig: WASD or arrows
  • Dig down: C
  • Dig up: <space>
  • Quick-Scan: Q

Requirements

Best played on a desktop or laptop with at least 1920x1080 resolution
