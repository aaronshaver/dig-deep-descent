Dig: Deep Descent

pre-alpha 0.2

Story

  • You scraped together funds to purchase a modest mining vessel.
  • After months of uneventful flight, you arrived at the Outer Belt.
  • Your goal is to sell enough minerals to acquire 1 billion Credits. That's enough to secure a coveted berth on an Interstellar Fleet ship. You want to escape this war-torn system.
  • You made contact with the Outer Belt Trading League. They will buy minerals from you. They will also sell upgrades, repairs, and fast-charging to your ship's batteries -- but only at the surface level!
  • As you dig deeper, more valuable minerals will appear, but so will more danger.

Controls

Move + Dig WASD, arrow keys
Dig Down / Descend C
Dig Up / Ascend <space>
Analyze Minerals Q

Requirements

Best played on a desktop or laptop with at least 1920x1080 resolution
